## Team Norms & Guidelines

### Working Agreement
1. **Respect everyone's time** - Start and end meetings on schedule
2. **Be present** - Active participation in meetings (cameras on when possible)
3. **Communicate proactively** - Share blockers and updates early
4. **Collaborate openly** - Ask questions, share knowledge
5. **Deliver quality** - Take ownership of your work
6. **Document decisions** - Keep project files up to date

### Code of Conduct
- Treat all team members with respect and professionalism
- Practice active listening and constructive feedback
- Support each other's growth and learning
- Celebrate successes and learn from failures together
- Maintain confidentiality of project information

## Escalation Path

**Issue Resolution:**
1. **Level 1:** Discuss within the team
2. **Level 2:** Escalate to Project Manager (Ashley)
3. **Level 3:** Escalate to Steering Committee/Sponsor

**Response Times:**
- **Critical Issues:** Within 1 hour
- **High Priority:** Within 4 hours
- **Medium Priority:** Within 1 business day
- **Low Priority:** Within 2 business days
