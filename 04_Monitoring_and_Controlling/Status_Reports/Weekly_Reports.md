## Reporting Guidelines

**Frequency**: Every Friday by 5:00 PM  
**Distribution**: All team members and stakeholders  
**Format**: Update this document with current week's information

**Required Sections**:
1. Summary - Brief overview of the week
2. Accomplishments - What was completed
3. In Progress - Active work items
4. Blockers - Issues preventing progress
5. Upcoming - Next week's priorities
6. Metrics - Quantitative progress indicators
7. Notes - Key decisions and observations
